All About Countries

A country is a distinct part of the world, such as a state,
nation, or other political entity. It may be a sovereign
state or make up one part of a larger state.[1] For example, the country of Japan is an independent, sovereign state, while the
country of Wales is a component of a multi-part sovereign state, the United Kingdom. A country may be a historically sovereign area
(such as Korea), a currently sovereign territory with a unified government (such as Senegal), or a non-sovereign geographic region
associated with certain distinct political, ethnic, or cultural characteristics (such as the Basque Country).
